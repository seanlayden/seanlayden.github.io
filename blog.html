Adventures in Programming

Hi! Welcome to my blog!

I'm fairly new to programming - this personal website is the first thing that I ever built on my own. What I want to accomplish with this blog is simple - I want it to be a resource for all of the other true beginners out there. I will keep as detailed of a log as possible of all of the resources that I use, the decisions that I make, and the projects that I am working on.

One thing that I have noticed about the programming community is that it is very open to beginners - ask for help, and chances are someone will respond positively and give you their real opinion. The best way to learn, in my opinion, is from experience - be it your own, or of others. However, I have also noticed that the industry (like many others) has a lot of jargon that sometimes gets skipped over. These are the sorts of things that are all over the place, and I had to do a lot of digging to find out exactly what they meant.
